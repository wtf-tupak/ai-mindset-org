## Structural Patterns

### Perfect Structure (AI признак):

**Типичная AI структура:**
```
Introduction (всегда есть чёткое введение)
  ↓
Body paragraph 1 (с transition: "First,")
  ↓
Body paragraph 2 (с transition: "Furthermore,")
  ↓
Body paragraph 3 (с transition: "Moreover,")
  ↓
Conclusion (всегда "In conclusion," или "To summarize,")
```

**Человеческий текст:**
- Неровная структура
- Иногда нет чёткого intro
- Conclusion может отсутствовать
- Длина параграфов варьируется
- Спонтанные отступления

---

### Transition Overload:

**AI использует в КАЖДОМ параграфе:**
- "Furthermore,"
- "Moreover,"
- "Additionally,"
- "In addition,"
- "Nevertheless,"
- "Nonetheless,"
- "Consequently,"
- "Hence,"

**Человеческий текст:**
Простые связки:
- "And"
- "But"
- "So"
- "Also"
- Или вообще без transition

---

### List Mania:

AI любит списки определённой длины:
- "Here are 5 ways..."
- "3 key points..."
- "7 strategies..."
- "Top 10 tips..."

**Признак AI:** Слишком много идеально структурированных списков

**Человеческий текст:**
- Списки разной длины
- Не всегда numbered
- Иногда в prose без списков

---

## Tonal Patterns

### Безликость:

❌ **AI признаки:**
- Нет "я" / "мы"
- Нет личного опыта
- Абстрактные примеры ("a company might...")
- Нет эмоций
- Нет мнений (только "facts")

✅ **Человеческий:**
- "Я сделал X и вот что..."
- "Мы обнаружили..."
- Конкретные примеры (Airbnb, Apple)
- Эмоции ("honestly", "блин", "surprisingly")
- Личные мнения

---

### Чрезмерная формальность:

❌ **AI признаки:**
- "It is" вместо "It's"
- "Do not" вместо "Don't"
- "Cannot" вместо "Can't"
- "Will not" вместо "Won't"
- Нет сокращений ВООБЩЕ

✅ **Человеческий:**
- Сокращения везде (it's, don't, can't, won't)
- Разговорный тон
- Иногда фрагменты. Как этот.

---

### Fake Enthusiasm:

❌ **AI признаки:**
- Слишком много восклицаний!!!
- "Exciting!"
- "Amazing!"
- "Incredible!"
Но при этом безликий тон (парадокс)

✅ **Человеческий:**
- Enthusiasm с личностью
- "Holy shit, this works!"
- Authentic emotion

---

## Sentence Patterns

### Contrast Framing Overload:

**Паттерн:** "It's not about X, it's about Y"

**AI использует 5+ раз:**
- "It's not about X, it's about Y"
- "The key isn't X, but rather Y"
- "Instead of X, focus on Y"
- "Rather than X, consider Y"

**1-2 раза** = ok
**5+ раз** = AI

---

### -ing Verb Overuse (Present Participle):

**AI любит начинать предложения с -ing:**
- "Ensuring quality..."
- "Providing value..."
- "Maintaining standards..."
- "Optimizing performance..."
- "Leveraging technology..."
- "Navigating complexities..."

**Человеческий:**
Активные глаголы:
- "Ensure quality"
- "Provide value"
- "Maintain standards"

---

### Vague Hedging:

**AI постоянно hedge:**
- "It's worth considering that..."
- "One might argue..."
- "It could be said..."
- "Some experts believe..."
- "Research suggests..."
- "This may indicate..."

**Человеческий:**
Прямо и уверенно:
- "This is..."
- "The data shows..."
- "I believe..."

## Detection Algorithm

### Scoring система:

**Старт:** 5.0 (perfect human score)

**Вычитаем:**
- Каждый Red Flag = -1.0
- Каждый Yellow Flag = -0.5
- Structural pattern = -0.5
- Tonal pattern = -0.5
- Sentence pattern = -0.3

**Финальный score:** 0-5

**Интерпретация:**
- 5.0 = 100% human
- 4.0-4.9 = Very natural (minor AI hints)
- 3.0-3.9 = Acceptable (some AI patterns)
- 2.0-2.9 = Noticeable AI (many patterns)
- 1.0-1.9 = Obviously AI (typical AI style)
- 0-0.9 = 100% AI slop

---

### Пример детекции:

**Текст:**
"It's important to note that leveraging AI can help optimize your workflow. Furthermore, this approach facilitates enhanced productivity. Moreover, it represents a paradigm shift in how we navigate the complexities of modern business."

**Детекция:**
```
Red Flags:
├─ "navigate the complexities" (-1.0)
└─ Total: -1.0

Yellow Flags:
├─ "it's important to note that" (-0.5)
├─ "leveraging" (-0.5)
├─ "optimize" (-0.5)
├─ "facilitates" (-0.5)
├─ "enhanced" (-0.5)
└─ Total: -2.5

Structural:
├─ "Furthermore" + "Moreover" transition overload (-0.5)
└─ Total: -0.5

Tonal:
├─ No "I/we", безликость (-0.5)
├─ No contractions (-0.5)
└─ Total: -1.0

Sentence:
├─ "represents" symbolic language (-0.3)
└─ Total: -0.3

Final Score: 5.0 - 5.3 = 0 (capped at 0)
AI Slop: 0/5 ❌❌ 100% AI
```

## Recommendations по фиксу

### Lexical fixes:

**BEFORE:**
"We need to utilize this approach to leverage our capabilities and optimize performance."

**AFTER:**
"We should use this approach to improve performance."

---

**BEFORE:**
"It's important to note that delving into these complexities requires a nuanced understanding."

**AFTER:**
"Look, this is complex. Here's what you need to know."

---

### Structural fixes:

**BEFORE:**
```
Furthermore, we see significant improvements.
Moreover, this indicates positive trends.
Additionally, the data shows growth.
```

**AFTER:**
```
We see big improvements.
This shows positive trends.
And the data confirms growth.
```

---

### Tonal fixes:

**BEFORE:**
"It is essential that one must consider the implications before proceeding."

**AFTER:**
"Here's the thing - think it through before you start."

---

**BEFORE:**
"Do not hesitate to contact us if you have questions."

**AFTER:**
"Don't hesitate to contact us if you've got questions."

---

### Add personality:

**BEFORE (Generic):**
"A company implemented this strategy and saw results."

**AFTER (Specific):**
"Airbnb tried this in 2018 and revenue jumped 40%."

---

**BEFORE (No emotion):**
"This approach was successful."

**AFTER (With emotion):**
"Honestly, this worked way better than expected."

---

**BEFORE (Perfect sentences):**
"The strategy was effective. It produced measurable results. We recommend implementing it."

**AFTER (Fragments + variety):**
"The strategy worked. Really well, actually. Measurable results across the board. Should you try it? Absolutely."

→ **Скорее всего human text**
